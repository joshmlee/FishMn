About this site & how to read the data

Built by a lifelong Minnesota angler who wanted an easier way to find great fishing lakes using real data. All survey data comes directly from the MN DNR LakeFinder program. My hope is that this tool helps fellow anglers discover new waters and deepen their appreciation for Minnesota's incredible fisheries.

CPUE

Catch Per Unit Effort — the number of fish caught per net per night. The most reliable way to compare fish abundance across lakes. Higher is better.

Standard Gill Nets

The DNR's primary survey method for walleye, pike, and perch. Consistent across lakes and years, making it the gold standard for ranking.

↑ Trend ↓ Trend

Compares the most recent survey to the previous one for your selected metric. Green means the population is up, red means it's down.

Date Filter

Limits results to lakes with recent survey data. Use "Last 5 years" to find lakes with the most current information.
